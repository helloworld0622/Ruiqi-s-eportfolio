Using DDPG to obtain optimal strategy in stock market

This project focuses on the use of Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient (DDPG) to develop an adaptive trading strategy. By training a DDPG agent with the daily prices of 30 select stocks to maximize investment returns.

GitHub

Project Overview

In the dynamic and complex stock market, leveraging strategy optimization is becoming increasingly important. The stock market can be modeled as a Markov Decision Process (MDP) with a focus on continuous and stochastic spaces. This project applied Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient (DDPG) for developing optimal trading strategies in the stock market. DDPG, known for its efficiency in handling high-dimensional, continuous action spaces, is particularly suited for portfolio allocation strategy of 30 DJIA stocks. The project explores the algorithm, detailing its algorithmic pseudocode, learning process, and the behavior of learned policy. The DDPG strategy outperformed the DJIA index not only in higher return but also in lower risk during the trade period in this study. It is recommended to continue refining the DDPG model with more comprehensive data and varying market scenarios.

The line graph compares the cumulative returns for the DDPG trading strategy and the DJIA index from November 1, 2021, to December 1, 2023. It illustrates the DDPG perform better than the benchmark index after first 3 months of the trade period. According to observation of all metrics, the DDPG strategy outperformed the DJIA index not only in higher return but also in lower risk. This DDPG strategy achieve optimal portfolio allocation by maximizing returns at a set risk level and minimizing risk.
